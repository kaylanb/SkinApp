Welcome to the Skin vs. Food Web App!

Instructions

  1. Click on the 4 Links at the top to navigate around
  2. Click on "Show_Test_Data"
    1. This shows 1 of 200 images (selected randomly) from our Test Data Set. Both "HOG" and "Blob" classifiers were trained on the same 300 image Training Data Set.
    2. You select what you see in the image, hit the "analyze" button, and the HOG/Blob results are output.
    3. To keep doing this for new images, hit the "yes" button at bottom
  3. Click on "Upload_Local_Image"
    1. Upload an image from you computer, select what you see in the image, hit "analyze" button
    2. WAIT for ~ 10-20 sec, it runs the trained HOG and Blob classifiers on your image so takes a bit, then HOG and Blob results output, and additional info for how each classifier works
